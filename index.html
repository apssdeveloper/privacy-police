1. Overview

Americas Cardroom is designed to operate entirely offline. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive information. All gameplay data, preferences, and achievements are stored locally on your device and never sent to any external server or third party.

We do not use analytics tools, ad networks, trackers, or cloud-based services of any kind.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address, or phone number)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts, calendar, or biometric data
  • Advertising IDs or usage analytics
  • Any data outside the app’s local sandbox
In short: the app cannot and does not access, transfer, or sell any user data.

3. Local Data Storage

All app data — including preferences, achievements, and unlocks — is stored securely on your device’s internal storage. You may delete it anytime via system settings or by uninstalling the app. No data ever leaves your device.

4. Permissions

The App requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location.

  • Local storage — used only for saving progress.
  • Notifications — optional local reminders (enabled manually).

All permissions are optional and can be disabled without affecting core functionality.

5. No Internet Connectivity

The app operates completely offline. It does not connect to the Internet, send or receive data, or interact with third-party APIs. All features — including gameplay, stats, and story — function without connectivity.

6. No Ads, No Tracking, No Purchases

  • No advertisements
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No in-app purchases
  • No social sharing or online components

Your experience is 100% private and free from external tracking or monetization.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for a general audience and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (US), under 16 (EU), or the local age of majority.

Because no data collection occurs, it complies with COPPA, the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code, and similar global laws.

8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA)

Although no data is collected, you retain full privacy rights, including:

  • Access & Portability: Export your data from within the app.
  • Correction & Deletion: Delete data via settings or uninstalling.
  • Transparency: You have full visibility — the app collects none.

9. Data Retention & Security

The app stores data only on your device within Android’s sandbox. We cannot access, recover, or modify any user data. Please secure your device with screen lock, encryption, or biometric protection.
