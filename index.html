1. Overview

Americas Cardroom is designed to operate entirely offline. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive information.

All gameplay data, preferences, and achievements are stored locally on your device and never sent to any external server or third party. We do not use analytics tools, ad networks, trackers, or cloud-based services of any kind.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or process any of the following types of data:

  • Personal identifiers (such as name, email, address, or phone number)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Biometric information
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or usage analytics
  • Any data stored on your device outside of the app’s own local sandbox

In short: the app cannot and does not access, transfer, or sell any user data.

3. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the app (such as scores, preferences, achievements, and unlocks) is saved locally on your device’s internal storage.

You may delete this data at any time by uninstalling the application or using your device’s system settings to clear app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not visible or accessible to us.

4. Permissions

The App requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access contacts, photos, microphone, camera, files, or location.

Some optional permissions may be used only for:

  • Local storage — to save user progress and configuration files;
  • Notifications — for local reminders (if enabled manually by the user).

All permissions remain optional and may be disabled at any time in your device’s settings. Disabling them will not affect core functionality.

5. No Internet Connectivity

Americas Cardroom is a fully offline experience. The App does not connect to the Internet, does not send or receive data, and does not interact with any third-party APIs or web services. All features, including statistics, story content, and challenges, work without an Internet connection.

6. No Ads, No Tracking, No Purchases

The App contains:

  • No advertisements (including personalized or contextual ads)
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No in-app purchases
  • No social sharing or online components

Your experience is 100% private and free from any external tracking or monetization.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and does not intentionally collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (United States), under 16 (European Union), or under the age of majority in their jurisdiction.

Because the app does not collect or transmit data, it complies by design with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code, and equivalent laws in the EU and Canada.

8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA)

Although no personal data is collected, users are entitled under law to certain rights regarding privacy and information control. These rights include:

  • Access and Portability: You may export your locally stored data within the app (e.g., backup/export feature).
  • Correction and Deletion: You may delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing its local storage.
  • Transparency: You have the right to know exactly what data (if any) is collected — in this case, none.

Since we do not process or control any user data on external servers, these rights are fully respected by design.

9. Data Retention

The App does not store data on our systems. All data exists only on your device and remains there until you choose to delete it. We cannot access, recover, or modify user data once deleted.

10. Security

The App operates within the Android sandbox environment, which isolates its data from other applications. No external transmission occurs.

Users are responsible for securing their devices (e.g., screen lock, encryption, or biometric protection) to prevent unauthorized local access.
